Overall Reflection


I believe my writing has improved from the beginning of the year due to direction from my teacher. The focus on close reading analysis helped me tremendously. I feel confident for English class next year and writing my college essay. The grammar skills I have learned will help me now and into the future. At the beginning of the year, I set a goal to improve my writing through focusing on analysis and thought organization. I can say now after reflecting that I have accomplished my goal. My writing is by no means perfect but I am satisfied with my improvement this year. I can say for sure the curriculum this year helped me improve every aspect of my writing. For instance, The vocab lessons were invaluable for the SATs and ACTs. Words such as “quintessence” and “opulent” appeared often and without my prior knowledge, I would not have known. In addition, the materials my teacher provided, Methods of Eliminating Wordiness etc., for our class to improve our class were extremely helpful. However, I do not think I did the best that I could in English this year. If I could do a few things differently, I would spend more time revising my writing, and I would constantly look back at the rubric/follow directions.
