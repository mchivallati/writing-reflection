Assignment Description


  • SWBAT effectively introduce a topic in an informative/explanatory work
  • SWBAT organize complex ideas and information, so that each element builds to create a unified whole, including formatting, graphics, and multimedia when necessary
  • SWBAT use relevant evidence to support a topic
  • SWBAT use a variety of transitions to connect elements of informative writing
  • SWBAT utilize vocabulary appropriate to informational topic and devices
  • SWBAT write in a formal tone adhering to conventions appropriate to discipline
  • SWBAT provide a logical conclusion
  • SWBAT tailor writing appropriately for task, purpose, and audience
  • SWBAT develop and strengthen writing by planning, rewriting, revising, and editing
  • SWBAT publish and update writing products through the use of technology
  • SWBAT include new arguments or information in response to feedback on published work
  • SWBAT write for a range of time frames, addressing a variety of tasks, purposes, and audiences
  • SWBAT recognize and correct grammatical errors
  • SWBAT express him/herself through personal and creative writing
  • SWBAT manage time effectively to complete a multi-step project on a timeline
