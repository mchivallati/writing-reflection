Comma Errors


Ethics/Morals Essay

Morals nearly mirror ethics but morals are internal.
Morals nearly mirror ethics, but morals are internal.
The first sentence was wrong because the “but” was trying to conjoin two independent clauses without a comma.

Ethics/Morals Essay

Morals change similar to ethics when an event causes a reaction by an individual, changing their beliefs.
Morals change, similar to ethics, when an event causes a reaction by an individual, changing their beliefs.
The phrase “similar to ethics” is extra information and should be separated by two commas, forming an interjection, to not confuse the reader.

Ethics/Morals Essay

Next tier down Organizations/Social Groups also impact a person’s internal views.
Next tier down, Organizations/Social Groups also impact a person’s internal views.
The clause “Next tier down” is a fragment or incomplete thought, and therefore must be separated by a comma.

Hamlet In-Class Essay

However, with a broader lense, Claudius’ quote also pertains to all of his conversations with other characters.
However with a broader lense, Claudius’ quote also pertains to all of his conversations with other characters.
Having a comma after however creates a choppy rhythm to the sentence, confusing readers.

Hamlet In-Class Essay

After failures to obtain his revenge on Claudius Hamlet vows during the final Acts for his “thoughts be bloody, or be nothing worth!” (Shakespeare IV.iv.66).
After failures to obtain his revenge on Claudius, Hamlet vows during the final Acts for his “thoughts be bloody, or be nothing worth!” (Shakespeare IV.iv.66).
The first clause is a fragment and needs to be connected to a completed clause with a comma.

Hamlet In-Class Essay

Tiring of failing in his sworn duty, Hamlet fully commits himself to vengeance, and isolates himself from the other characters, by not conforming with the societal norm of seeking power.
Tiring of failing in his sworn duty, Hamlet fully commits himself to vengeance, and isolates himself from the other characters; not conforming with the societal norm of seeking power.
The comma before “by” makes no sense here. To fix the sentence, I added a semicolon to eliminate the run-on sentence and show a connection between two clauses.

Catcher In The Rye Essay

This confusion may also lead to poor life decisions resonating throughout the rest of an adolescent’s life.
This confusion may also lead to poor life decisions, resonating throughout the rest of an adolescent’s life.
You need a comma before “resonating” to connect the independent clause with the dependent clause.

Gatsby Essay

In reality, Gatsby only wants Daisy for her status but he can never win her.
In reality, Gatsby only wants Daisy for her status, but he can never win her.
“But” tries to conjoin an independent and dependent clause without a comma.

Gatsby Essay

Through the relationship of Gatsby and Daisy, Fitzgerald critics nineteen twenties culture by letting Gatsby lose Daisy, and showing Daisy understands the world around her.
Through the relationship of Gatsby and Daisy, Fitzgerald critics nineteen twenties culture by letting Gatsby lose Daisy; showing Daisy understands the world around her.
Adding a semicolon stops the sentence from being a run-on, and the semicolon separates two clauses while keeping the relation between them strong. This eliminates the reader’s confusion.

Gatsby Essay

After failures to obtain his revenge on Claudius Hamlet vows during the final Acts for his “thoughts be bloody, or be nothing worth!” (Shakespeare IV.iv.66).
After failures to obtain his revenge on Claudius, Hamlet vows during the final Acts for his “thoughts be bloody, or be nothing worth!” (Shakespeare IV.iv.66).
The first clause is a fragment and needs to be connected to a completed clause with a comma.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Eliminating Wordiness


Gatsby Essay

Daisy uses the word “hope” to express the magnitude of her suffering, for there cannot be despair without hope.
Daisy uses the word “hope” to express her suffering because there cannot be despair without hope.
Eliminate unnecessary determiners and modifiers

Ethics/Morals Essay

To change an ethical climate, people must stick up for what they believe is right.
People must fight for their views to change an ethical climate.
Change Passive Verbs into Active Verbs

Ethics/Morals Essay

Internal meaning that morals are specific to a certain individual.
Internal meaning the morals of one person.
Change unnecessary that, who, and which clauses into phrases

Catcher In Essay

Internal meaning that morals are specific to a certain individual.
Internal meaning the morals of one person.
Change unnecessary that, who, and which clauses into phrases

Catcher In The Rye Essay

In truth, Holden’s behavior mirrors that of a child at times, which further distances him from his peers.
Holden’s behavior mirrors that of a child at times, further distancing him from his peers.
Eliminate words that explain the obvious or provide excessive detail
